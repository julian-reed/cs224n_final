Why dost thou pine within and suffer dearth,

Two loves I have, of comfort and despair,
That like two spirits do suggest me still.
My better angel is a man right fair, yet wicked
In the offing we celebrate.
Is this good because thou right stand'st nought?
Were I then myself false and beguiled to thee?

Flattery, to but degrade beauty?
